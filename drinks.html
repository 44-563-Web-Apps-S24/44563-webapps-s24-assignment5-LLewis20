Lemonade is a refreshing citrus drink made from lemon juice, water, and sweetener. It is a popular summer beverage and can be enjoyed on its own or mixed with other ingredients to create various cocktails.

Eggnog

Eggnog is a rich and creamy holiday drink made with milk, cream, sugar, beaten eggs, and flavored with nutmeg and vanilla. It is often spiked with alcohol such as rum or brandy and served chilled.
